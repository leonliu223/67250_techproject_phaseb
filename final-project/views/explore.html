Our Mission

MonoMuse exists to bridge the gap between art and technology, making cutting-edge creative experiences accessible to everyone. We believe that art has the power to illuminate the human condition, and that technology opens new dimensions for storytelling, expression, and connection. Our mission is to inspire curiosity, foster innovation, and cultivate a deep appreciation for creativity in all its forms.

Who We Serve

MonoMuse welcomes visitors of all ages and backgrounds — from students and educators to professional artists, technologists, and lifelong learners. Our programming is designed to engage curious minds at every level, with dedicated experiences for school groups, families, and adult audiences.

Our History

Founded in 2008, MonoMuse began as a small experimental gallery in Pittsburgh focused on digital art and interactive media. Over the years it has grown into a nationally recognized contemporary museum with a permanent collection spanning generative art, human-computer interaction, and immersive installation.

Milestones

  • 2008 — MonoMuse founded as a digital art gallery in Pittsburgh
  • 2015 — Expanded to include a permanent collection of generative art
  • 2025 — Launched the Interactive Innovation Lab for hands-on visitor experiences
